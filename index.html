Indoor Plants

Indoor plants are a common favorite of beginner botonist who wish to capture the spirit of nature. These plants are usually succulents, and require very little maintenance to handle while adding a lively wilderness to any setting

Outdoor Plants

Outdoor plants are a common favorite of a serious botonist who has grown to handle the variety of plants nature has to offer. Though difficult to handle and maintain, their illustrious colors and beauty captures the soul of all those who look upon them
